Instrument Catalog

Electric Guitar

Recommendation: Electric guitars are popular and versatile instruments suitable for various music genres.

Price Range:

  • Entry-level: $150 - $500
  • Mid-range: $500 - $1,500
  • High-end: $1,500 and above

Digital Piano

Recommendation: Digital pianos are space-efficient and offer a range of sounds, making them suitable for beginners and professionals.

Price Range:

  • Entry-level: $300 - $800
  • Mid-range: $800 - $2,000
  • High-end: $2,000 and above

Drum Kit

Recommendation: Full drum kits appeal to drummers of all skill levels and are essential for various music styles.

Price Range:

  • Starter kit: $300 - $700
  • Intermediate kit: $700 - $1,500
  • Professional kit: $1,500 and above

Saxophone

Recommendation: Saxophones, especially alto or tenor, are versatile wind instruments suitable for various genres, including jazz and classical.

Price Range:

  • Student model: $500 - $1,200
  • Intermediate model: $1,200 - $2,500
  • Professional model: $2,500 and above
